Game Development

I’ve been developing games for the past five years, with a focus on combining conceptually interesting systems games with mechanically satisfying, fast-paced action style games. I’m interested in joining a company or team as a gameplay, UI, or systems programmer. Below is a sample of some of my work:

Ongoing Projects

Sun Spear
   2023
Play Github

Sun Spear began as a student project created by a team of four over the course of ten weeks, and we plan to release it on steam by fall 2023. This 3rd person 3D adventure game follow Bonnie as she battles against the forces of evil and darkness in order to save her village.

Bonnie has a magical spear that can be thrown or used in melee range, and there is an explosion ability which can damage enemies and throw them back. Over the course of the ~60 minute playtime, the player will face off against four distinct enemy types and three bosses that stand in their way. The game plays out over the course of a single day, with the aesthetics, post-processing, and lighting cycling through four different times.

I was responsible for all of the programming and animation implementation for this project. There were a number of interesting challenges, from the enemy AI design and bosses to the time-of-day changes, a custom framework for playing various kind of sound file, and a robust save system - it’s been an interesting project to work on.
